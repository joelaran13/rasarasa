Our Story

Simple Food,
Big Flavors.

Located in the heart of Sibu, Rasa Rasa isn't just a restaurant; it's a tribute to the comfort of home cooking. We stripped away the pretension and focused on what matters: taste.

Whether it's the crunch of our fried chicken skin or the warmth of our butter sauce, every element is designed to make you close your eyes and savor the moment.
